Passion, Desire & Interest

Graphic Design Experience

I have been a graphic designer for 8 years, in my years of experience i have designed logos in English and Arabic. Utilising my digital skills, drawing skills and typography skills in logo design, i decided to move on to branding design. Thereafter, this tied me into webdesign, designing using front end tools, i learnt HTML & CSS. My Graphic Design skills include Adobe Ps, Illustartor, XD, InDesign and as of recent, Dimension. I have created branding for clients globally and have designed clothing as well. As of now i design Creative Conceptions, being the head of social media content and also marketing/branding lead. Ensuring that all brands we have are correctly marketed and branded online on all platforms, particularly Amazon and our own website as well as on social media. I have designed for a range of companies in different industries, such as construction, house repairs, roofers, landscapers, restaurants, hotels and many more. Furthermore, i have designed for the adult novelty gifts and also sexual health industries.
